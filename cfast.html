About Daisy Pictures Studio

Daisy Pictures Studio was born from a passion for pushing the boundaries of cinematic technology. As filmmakers ourselves, we intimately understood the challenges faced by professionals in the field. The genesis of DP CINE CFast and CFexpress cards came from our firsthand experiences with the limitations of existing storage solutions.

Our journey began when we encountered data loss during a crucial shoot due to unreliable media. This pivotal moment sparked a vision: to create storage solutions that could withstand the rigors of professional production environments while delivering uncompromising performance.

We assembled a team of industry veterans and cutting-edge engineers, embarking on a mission to revolutionize digital cinema storage. Years of research, development, and field testing culminated in the DP CINE line of CFast and CFexpress cards.

Our designs prioritize three key factors:

  • Ironclad reliability to safeguard irreplaceable footage
  • Thermal efficiency to maintain peak performance in challenging conditions

Today, DP CINE cards are trusted by top productions worldwide, from Hollywood blockbusters to cutting-edge independent films. We continue to innovate, staying ahead of evolving camera technology to ensure that creatives always have the tools they need to bring their visions to life.
