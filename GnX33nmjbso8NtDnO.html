TAX INCENTIVES

INTERNATIONAL PRODUCTIONS

Unlock substantial financial benefits with our industry-leading 50% tax rebate on eligible expenditure for international productions in the Canary Islands.

  • Minimum spend: €1 million
  • Maximum rebate: €18 million
  • Eligible formats: Feature films, TV series, Animation, Documentaries
  • No cultural test required

SPANISH PRODUCTIONS

Benefit from one of Europe's most competitive tax incentives with our 45-50% tax credit for national productions.

  • 45% for the first €1 million, 50% for amounts exceeding €1 million
  • Minimum spend: €200,000 for animation and short films, €1 million for others
  • Maximum credit: €18 million
  • Additional access to national and regional funding schemes

CANARY ISLANDS SPECIAL ZONE (ZEC)

Maximize your production's financial efficiency with an incredibly low 4% corporate tax rate in the Canary Islands Special Zone.

  • Minimum investment: €100,000 in fixed assets within two years
  • Employment requirement: Creation of at least 5 full-time jobs
  • Validity: Benefits applicable until 2026, with potential extension
  • Can be combined with other incentives
