CFexpress Type B: The Future of High-Capacity Storage for Professional Cinematography

Why CFexpress is Revolutionizing the Film Industry

CFexpress Type B cards have become the gold standard for professional cinematographers and videographers worldwide. These cutting-edge storage solutions offer unparalleled capacity, speed, and reliability, making them essential for modern high-resolution and high-bitrate video workflows.

Key Advantages of CFexpress Type B Technology

  • Massive storage capacity (up to 4TB)
  • Blazing fast read and write speeds (up to 2100MB/s)
  • Sustained performance for 8K RAW video recording
  • Robust build quality for demanding shooting conditions
  • Advanced thermal management for consistent performance
  • Compatibility with leading cinema camera brands

CFexpress vs Other Storage Solutions

When compared to other storage options like CFast 2.0 or SD cards, CFexpress Type B cards offer significant advantages in terms of capacity, speed, and reliability. This makes them the preferred choice for professional productions, especially those involving 8K resolution, high frame rates, or multi-camera setups.

Choosing the Right CFexpress Card for Your Needs

When selecting a CFexpress card, consider factors such as your camera's compatibility, required capacity for your typical shooting duration, and the bitrate of your preferred recording format. DP Cine Cards offer a range of options to suit various professional needs, from the 1TB model for standard productions to the 4TB powerhouse for the most demanding workflows.
