Frequently Asked Questions

How do DP Cine Cards compare to other CFexpress Type B cards?

DP Cine Cards offer industry-leading capacity, speed, and reliability. With options up to 4TB and read speeds up to 2100MB/s, they outperform most competitors in the market.

Are DP Cine Cards compatible with my camera?

DP Cine Cards are compatible with a wide range of professional cameras that support CFexpress Type B, including models from ARRI, Canon, RED, Sony, and more. Check our compatibility list or contact our support team for specific model compatibility.

What kind of warranty do DP Cine Cards come with?

All DP Cine Cards come with a 5-year warranty. Our 2TB and 4TB models also include priority support and next-day replacement options.

How do I ensure the best performance and longevity for my DP Cine Card?

To maximize performance and lifespan, always use the card with compatible devices, avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures or moisture, and use our recommended card readers for data transfer. Regularly update your camera's firmware and format the card in-camera before use.

Can DP Cine Cards handle high-bitrate 8K RAW recording?

Yes, DP Cine Cards are specifically designed to handle the demands of 8K RAW recording. With sustained write speeds of up to 1800MB/s and VPG400 certification, they can easily manage high-bitrate 8K workflows.
